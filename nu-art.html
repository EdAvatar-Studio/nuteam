Artist Profile

NU ART

Personal works from real-world making.

Nu Art is the personal visual direction of Wisanu Saengwirun — rooted in sculpture, texture, carving, and large-scale physical construction, then extended into experiments, studies, and evolving digital presentation.

This page is not only a gallery of finished works. It is a view into artistic thinking, material language, and form built through real experience.

Artist Statement

BUILT FROM REAL WORK

Nu Art comes from years of working with real scale, real surfaces, and real environments. The artistic mindset was not built only in studios, but also through construction sites, international projects, and material problem-solving.

Direction

FROM PHYSICAL TO DIGITAL

The direction now expands from physical work into digital systems — not to replace the real, but to extend it. Art, tools, AI, and presentation can live together under the same identity.
